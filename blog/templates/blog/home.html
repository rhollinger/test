about us

#TriTechElite is a team of post-collegiate emerging triathletes training and racing throughout New England.

The squad was formed in 2011 as a training group for multisport athletes studying at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. What started as a Twitter hashtag quickly caught momentum and a team was born. The team saw quick growth collecting athletes from the Varsity Track, Cross Country, Cycling, and Swimming programs to share the multisport passion.


Currently the team is composed of over 10 full-time engineers. The team takes pride in the careful balance of professional careers and multi-sport training. Still #TriTechElite has found great success, racing to the top of the New England circuit and achieving numerous All-American distinctions on the national stage.
